Breath of the Wild has one of the most unique crafting systems. Unlike most games that gives you some direction, Breath of the Wild lets you grab food and combine it without any recipes. If the individual ingredients that are added to the cook pot are complementary, then they will create food that can increase your attack power, health, damage resistance, and even eliminate the effects of hot and cold weather.
